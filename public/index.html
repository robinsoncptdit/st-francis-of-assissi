St. Francis of Assissi Catholic Church

Three Communities, 

Two Parishes, One Church

St. Francis of Assisi serves the rural community of Blountstown Florida. Among the priorities of our mission are providing a home of worship for both Spanish and English speaking people as well as ministering to the souls incarcerated in the prisons nearby.

Fr. Richard Schamber serves as our pastor, supported by his staff. They serve both St. Francis of Assisi Parish and our sister parish, Holy Cross Parish in Chattahoochee Florida.
